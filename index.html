What is an Online Dice Roller?

An online dice roller is a digital tool that simulates the random roll of physical dice. Also called a virtual dice roller, dice rolling app, or random dice roller, it allows you to roll dice online for free without needing physical dice. Our dice roller virtual tool supports all standard polyhedral dice used in tabletop games.

How Does Virtual Dice Work?

Virtual dice use a random number generator (RNG) to produce results equivalent to a physical dice roll. When you roll dice online, the algorithm generates a random integer between 1 and the number of sides — just like rolling the dice in real life. Our online dice roller is designed to be as fair and unpredictable as physical dice.

What is a 6-Sided Dice?

A 6-sided dice (also called a D6) is the most common type of die, used in games like Monopoly, Yahtzee, Backgammon, and Ludo. When you roll a 6-sided die, the result is a random number between 1 and 6. Our online dice roller lets you roll D6 dice — one at a time or in multiples — instantly for free.

Why Use a Dice Rolling App?

A dice rolling app is perfect when you don't have physical dice nearby, when playing games online with friends, or when you need to roll many dice quickly.
